Happy Programmer's Day

Details

The Day of the Programmer is an international professional day that is celebrated on the 256th day of each year in 13 september. It is officially recognized in Russia. The number 256 was chosen because it is the number of distinct values that can be represented with a byte, a value well known to programmers.
